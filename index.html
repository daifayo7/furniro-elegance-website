Our Craft

A Legacy of
Master Artisans

We partner with century-old ateliers in France and Italy. Each piece undergoes dozens of meticulous steps, shaped by master craftspeople. From timber selection to final polishing, every stage reflects our obsession with perfection.

Our leather comes from Tuscany, metal hardware is hand-patinated, and solid wood is naturally air-dried for at least three years. This material commitment ensures furniture that ages gracefully.
